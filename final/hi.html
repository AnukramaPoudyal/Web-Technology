Answer

Answer, Answer, Question For someone like you And it would be like a shooting star I would be a reckless gambler But you know you're not what they think And I'm willing to bet There'd be an earthquake if I bet against you I'll bet all I have on you But it comes down to the final shot I'd do anything and everything to have got that dream come true All I tried to do to try not to let it get away To find out what I was thinking and what I was doing Who you are to be, who you are to be That's another question, another answer Answer, answer, question. You are to be, you are to be

Dropped

Now I've Dropped Everything I've fallen in love with you so easily You don't have to be scared of me Now I've dropped everything I've found a girl For this chance that we share You don't have to be scared of me I could see it in the way her eyes shone Let's have a past, let's kiss goodbye And as we smoke in the ashtrays I wonder if we'll ever run on this memory, oh Oh no, no, no Let's stop all this screaming And never ever leave on the past, oh I don't want to see The tears that nobody cries I don't want to feel the joy of love again Never get to feel my loneliness I just can't seem to get much closer Then I had hoped

Last Journey

In the shoes of my grandfather, I stand, Seeking solace in words, guided by his hand. Through these verses, I strive to comprehend, The journey he undertook, until his life's end. A grand symphony of life once did he weave, Full of vigor, strength, and passions to believe. Yet fate's cruel hand struck him with despair, An accident, a battle he fought, unfair. In these walls, his spirit did reside, Beneath pale lights, where hope and pain collide. But through it all, a resilience unfurled, His spirit, unyielding, in an uncertain world. Each day a struggle, a test of his might, Challenged by shadows that loomed in the night. Yet his spirit remained unbroken and strong, For love and family, he carried along. With every breath, he cherished life's embrace, Embracing memories, with a gentle grace. In his eyes, I saw the wisdom of years, The tales untold, hidden within his tears. A lifetime of stories whispered in his gaze, Of love, laughter, and wistful yesterdays. He taught me lessons, unspoken and wise, To live each moment, to see through love's eyes. And as the seasons danced, their colors unfurled, His soul yearned for freedom from this earthly world. A hush fell over his fragile silhouette, As he embarked on a journey he couldn't forget. With grace, he surrendered to the unknown, Embracing peace, his spirit softly flown. His essence remains, a guiding light, sublime, Forever etched in my heart, throughout time. So now, in these verses, I honor his name, His spirit enduring, his legacy aflame. Though his earthly journey has come to an end, His love and wisdom forever transcend. Through the words I pen, his story shall live, A testament of strength and the love he'd give. For in the shoes of my grandfather, I stand, Forever grateful, held by his guiding hand.

Nothing New

New year, same old lads Let's discuss our leaders, oh so great Who sit atop our nation, but we can't relate Why they can't keep their reputation clean and fair Instead they indulge in corruption, without a single care They make promises they can't fulfill, with a grin so wide But when it's time to deliver, they run and hide They deceive and manipulate, with no sense of remorse And we are the ones who have to bear the course Their words are so false, their deeds even more They don't care about us, only about their score They take and take, without giving back And yet they blame us for their own lack They say they'll fight corruption, but what do they do? They themselves engage in it, and make us feel blue They hold our future in their dirty hands And they manipulate it, like a game of sands But we won't fall for their lies, we'll rise above And we'll make sure they know, we deserve better love We'll stand up for our rights, and demand what's fair And we'll show them that their corruption, we won't bear So let's speak up, and make our voices heard Let's fight for what's right, with every single word And we'll ensure that our nation will see A brighter future, where corruption will cease to be So our beloved leaders, listen to us loud and clear We won't let you rob our future, year after year We'll work towards a better tomorrow, and we'll win And we'll show you that honesty and integrity will always win So as our New Year 2080 resolution, let's pledge to eradicate Corruption from our land, and make it a better state We'll work hard towards our goal, with every step we take And we'll make sure that our nation's future is at stake.

Procrastination Blues: A Student's Lament

Exams are coming near, but here I am Scrolling TikTok and Instagram, oh damn No energy to study or take notes Just endless scrolling, what a joke I say "I'll study," but that's cap End up watching reels, and feel like a sap I tell myself "just one more vid," But an hour goes by, and I feel like a kid The clock is ticking, the deadline draws near But I'm still here, scrolling without any fear Thinking I have time, that I can catch up But the reality is, I'm really screwed up I watch videos of cats and dogs Dance challenges, and silly pranks of all sorts Thinking it's just for a minute or two But hours fly by, what can I do? I know I should be studying, it's true But TikTok's algorithm's got me in its queue The reels keep coming, one after the other And before I know it, the exam's here, oh brother I try to cram, but my mind's all over the place Thinking of the videos, I've watched and their pace I get stressed, anxiety creeps in Wondering how I'm going to ace this thing And when the exam day finally arrives, I feel like a fool with hollow eyes, All night I cram and try to recall, The things I should have learned in fall. The morning comes with a dreadful sound, Of my alarm clock that I want to drown, I get dressed and head to school, Feeling like a clueless fool. And then the exam is over, and I feel so dumb Realizing that social media's not worth the fun I promise to myself, to study next time But I know I'll end up doing the same old crime So if you're like me, addicted to TikTok and Instagram Remember to put the phone down and stick to the plan Study hard, and don't let social media distract Or else you'll end up like me, feeling the exam regret.

Teen World

In the realm where wisdom seeks its home, Amidst the halls of knowledge, we roam. A tapestry of youthful dreams unfurled, The vibrant canvas of the student world. We gather at the threshold of our prime, Eager hearts beating in rhythm and rhyme. Books and pens become our loyal kin, As we embark on the journey to begin. Within these walls, a sanctuary to explore, Boundless horizons, we strive to soar. Lectures, labs, and endless debates, As we navigate the realm of fates. The morning sun awakens weary eyes, With coffee cups, we watch the dawn arise. Late nights spent, lost in endless toil, Seeking solace in the library's coil. With minds aflame, ideas interweave, In lecture halls where scholars conceive. Through words and numbers, equations dance, Unlocking secrets of life's cosmic expanse. The camaraderie we forge along the way, In study groups that linger past the day. Together, we conquer every challenge faced, A tapestry of souls, intricately laced. Exams loom, a tempest drawing near, Sleepless nights filled with doubt and fear. Yet resilience blossoms within our hearts, As we strive to showcase our finest arts. Amidst the stress, laughter finds its place, In shared jokes and moments we embrace. We celebrate achievements, big and small, Encouraging each other to stand tall. But life as a student is not all books, For friendships grow in hidden nooks. We revel in adventures, wild and free, Discovering who we truly long to be. So let us raise our voices in acclaim, To the student life that kindled our flame. For in its embrace, we found our way, Guided by the sun, through night and day. And when we leave these hallowed grounds, Our spirits soar, like birds unbound. With dreams ignited, we shall take flight, Fueled by the student life's eternal light.
